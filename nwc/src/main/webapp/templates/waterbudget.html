Available Water Budget Components

Discover and access precipitation and evapotranspiration data for watersheds and counties.
  1. Zoom to a your location of interest.
  2. Turn on the watersheds map layer.
  3. Select a watershed of interest.
  4. Water use data for counties intersecting the watershed are available on the next page.
Map Controls:
Single click selects sites or watershed. Pan by click and drag. Zoom with double click, map buttons or scroll wheel. Can pan by click and drag. Zoom with double click, map buttons or scroll wheel Click and hold to drag a bounding box to zoom.
Turn Watersheds Turn on/off map-display of Watershed Boundary Dataset for which water budget information is available.
