Water withdrawals by county area is represented as evenly distributed across the total area of the county. In reality, these water withdrawals are not evenly distributed. The water withdrawals shown may be concentrated in parts of the county which are outside the selected HUC, or may be concentrated in the area of the county which is in the selected HUC.
